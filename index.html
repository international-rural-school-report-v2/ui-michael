International Rural School Report

The premier equipment and issue tracking tool for schools around the world, enabling rapid communication and resolution of needs with partner school boards.

About IRSR

Our mission is to provide a reliable tool for school admins and board members to effectively communicate and resolve school issues. With the ability to track and schedule issues and their status, this application will provide a great mainstream workflow to benefit not just the board members and school admins, but the students too.
